Our Story

Two Decades of Smiles

Founded in 2004 by Dr. Ajay Agarwal, Dr. Agarwal's Dental Health Clinic & Implant Centre began as a single-chair practice with one mission: to make world-class dental care accessible to every family in Agra.

Over two decades, we've grown into one of Agra's most trusted multi-specialty dental centers, equipped with state-of-the-art technology and staffed by compassionate professionals who genuinely care about your oral health.

In 2010, Dr. Rachana Agarwal — an expert orthodontist — joined the clinic, broadening our specializations and bringing a holistic approach to smile design and dental wellness that has transformed thousands of lives.
