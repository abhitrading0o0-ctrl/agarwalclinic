About Dr. Ajay

A Surgeon Who Listens First

Dr. Ajay Agarwal founded Agarwal Dental Clinic in 2004 with a singular belief: that exceptional dental care must be accessible to every family in Agra, without compromise on quality or compassion.

With an MDS in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery from a premier Indian institution, Dr. Ajay brings surgical precision to every case — from a simple extraction to complex full-mouth rehabilitations. He is known among patients for his ability to completely eliminate dental anxiety through calm, detailed explanations before any procedure.

He has advanced training in implantology, rotary endodontics, and digital dentistry, keeping the clinic at the forefront of modern dental practice in Agra.

"Every patient deserves to leave our clinic not just with better teeth, but with the confidence to smile freely. That's what drives me every single day."
— Dr. Ajay Agarwal
