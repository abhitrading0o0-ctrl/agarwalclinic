About Dr. Rachana

Precision, Patience & Beautiful Smiles

Dr. Rachana Agarwal joined Agarwal Dental Clinic in 2010, bringing with her an MDS in Orthodontics that has since benefited thousands of patients across Agra and the surrounding region.

Her approach to orthodontics goes far beyond just straight teeth. Dr. Rachana views bite correction as a holistic discipline — one that impacts jaw health, breathing, chewing efficiency, and long-term oral wellbeing. She is particularly known for her work with children, making complex orthodontic procedures comfortable and even enjoyable for young patients.

She holds special certifications in clear aligner therapy and has trained extensively in aesthetic and digital smile design, enabling her to deliver orthodontic results that are as beautiful as they are functionally sound.

"I believe everyone — from a 7-year-old with a crowded bite to a 45-year-old wanting to finally fix their smile — deserves personalised, compassionate orthodontic care."
— Dr. Rachana Agarwal
