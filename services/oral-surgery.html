Surgical Services

Precise Care, Minimal Invasiveness

Dr. Ajay Agarwal's MDS in Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery means every procedure is performed with the highest level of surgical training in a sterile, fully-equipped environment.

Our oral surgery services include:

  • Wisdom tooth (3rd molar) extraction — simple & surgical
  • Impacted tooth exposure & removal
  • Dental cyst & abscess drainage
  • Frenectomy (tongue-tie & lip-tie release)
  • Pre-prosthetic surgery for denture preparation
  • Oral biopsy for suspicious lesions
  • Jaw trauma management
  • Implant-related bone grafting
