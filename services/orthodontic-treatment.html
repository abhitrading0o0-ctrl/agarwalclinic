About Orthodontics

Beautiful, Aligned Smiles for Every Age

Orthodontic treatment corrects teeth alignment and bite problems (malocclusion) that affect both appearance and oral health. Misaligned teeth are harder to clean and can cause wear, speech issues, and jaw pain over time.

Dr. Rachana Agarwal specialises in a full range of orthodontic appliances — choosing the best option based on your severity, lifestyle, and budget.

  • Metal braces — most effective, most affordable
  • Ceramic (tooth-coloured) braces — discreet option
  • Clear aligners (Invisalign-style) — near-invisible
  • Retainers fabricated for lifelong maintenance
  • Treats crooked teeth, gaps, overbite & underbite
  • Suitable for children (6+), teens, and adults

The Process

Your Orthodontic Journey

1

Initial Assessment

Comprehensive oral exam, dental photos, X-rays, and dental impressions to plan your treatment.

2

Appliance Fitting

Braces bonded or aligners fitted. You'll see your treatment timeline and expected results.

3

Monthly Adjustments

Regular visits every 4–6 weeks to adjust wires or swap aligners and monitor progress.

4

Retainer & Completion

Braces removed, custom retainer fitted to maintain your beautiful new alignment for life.
