About Whitening

Bright Smiles, Lasting Results

Professional teeth whitening uses regulated hydrogen or carbamide peroxide concentrations applied by trained professionals. The results are immediate, dramatic, and far safer than unregulated products.

We offer three whitening systems tailored to your goals, timeline, and sensitivity level:

  • In-Chair Zoom Whitening — 1 hour, up to 8 shades
  • Laser-Assisted Whitening — faster activation
  • Take-Home Kits — custom trays for gradual brightening
  • Safe for enamel — pH-balanced formulas
  • Gum protection shields used throughout
  • Results last 1–2 years with maintenance

The Process

In-Chair Whitening: What to Expect

1

Pre-Treatment Cleaning

Teeth cleaned to remove surface plaque for maximum whitening agent penetration.

2

Gum Shield Applied

Protective barrier placed to isolate gums from the whitening gel. Cheek retractors used.

3

Gel & Light Activation

Whitening gel applied, activated with LED or laser light in 3–4 cycles of 15 minutes.

4

Shade Assessment

Final shade compared against pre-treatment. Take-home kit provided for maintenance.
